GST Registration

Eligibility

  • Businesses with annual turnover exceeding ₹40 lakhs (₹10 lakhs for special category states)
  • E-commerce operators
  • Casual taxable persons
  • Non-resident taxable persons

Documents Required

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Business proof documents
  • Bank account details
  • Digital signature

Registration Steps

  1. Visit the GST portal
  2. Fill Part A of the registration form
  3. Submit required documents
  4. Complete Part B of the registration
  5. Submit the application
  6. Receive GSTIN after verification

Company Registration

Eligibility

  • Minimum 2 directors for private limited company
  • Minimum 7 directors for public limited company
  • Valid address proof
  • Minimum capital requirement as per company type

Documents Required

  • Director's PAN and Aadhaar
  • Address proof of directors
  • Registered office proof
  • Memorandum of Association
  • Articles of Association
  • Digital signature certificates

Registration Steps

  1. Obtain Digital Signature Certificate
  2. Apply for Director Identification Number
  3. Name approval application
  4. File incorporation documents
  5. Obtain Certificate of Incorporation

LLP Registration

Eligibility

  • Minimum 2 designated partners
  • At least one partner must be resident in India
  • Valid business address
  • No minimum capital requirement

Documents Required

  • Partner's PAN and Aadhaar
  • Address proof of partners
  • Registered office proof
  • LLP Agreement
  • Digital signature certificates

Registration Steps

  1. Obtain Digital Signature Certificate
  2. Apply for Designated Partner Identification Number
  3. Name approval application
  4. File incorporation documents
  5. Obtain Certificate of Incorporation

Import Export Code Registration

Eligibility

  • Any person/entity engaged in import/export
  • Valid PAN Card
  • Valid business address
  • Bank account details

Documents Required

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Address proof
  • Bank account details
  • Digital signature

Registration Steps

  1. Visit DGFT website
  2. Fill the application form
  3. Upload required documents
  4. Pay application fee
  5. Submit application
  6. Receive IEC code after verification

MSME Registration

Eligibility

  • Micro Enterprises: Investment up to ₹1 crore and turnover up to ₹5 crore
  • Small Enterprises: Investment up to ₹10 crore and turnover up to ₹50 crore
  • Medium Enterprises: Investment up to ₹50 crore and turnover up to ₹250 crore

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Business address proof
  • Bank account details
  • Business classification details

Registration Steps

  1. Visit Udyam Registration portal
  2. Fill the registration form
  3. Enter business details
  4. Submit required documents
  5. Receive Udyam Registration Number
